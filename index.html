Hello! Welcome to my github.

In 2018 I was in the first class of the Columbus State Community College's Workforce Innovation Data Analytics Certificate program. It was a partnership between my employer and the college. I found data analytics to be both exciting and challenging. This site contains sample projects used to continue my education with Data Analytics.

CSCC Workforce Innovation Data Analytics Certificate - Issued By Columbus State Community College

Individuals who earn the CSCC Workforce Innovation Data Analytics Certificate are able to translate business requirements into analytics needs, perform exploratory data analysis, clean and merge data from multiple sources, use notebooks to manipulate data, identify patterns, trends, and relationships in data, identify, apply and validate appropriate statistical models, and create visualizations and dashboards to present information.
